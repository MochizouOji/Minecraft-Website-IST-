Redstone

One of the most advanced mechanics in this sandbox game is redstone. It is able to accomplish many different complex contraptions such as a calculator, advanced deathtraps and even a working computer.

Simple Uses of Redstone:

  • Sorting items
  • Transporting players
  • Automatic door

When placed on the ground, redstone dust will become redstone wire which can be used to transfer power from a redstone torch or a switch such as a Lever or Button. This can be used to activate items such as Doors, Pistons, Note blocks, rails, or a handy trap. Redstone is the real equivalent of wires and electrical circuits; they don't go together with water but are heavily relied upon late game for big projects such as large automatic farms and item sorting systems. Things like the redstone repeater, calibrated sculk sensor, observer and tripwire hook are all commonly used items related to redstone contraptions.
