Getting Started
With over 300 million downloads and over 18 official versions, Minecraft is one of the most played and loved games of all time!

First released in 2009, the community has only been growing. Playable in both single and multi-player, this game, developed by Mojang, was aimed at all audiences. The classic version of this game is in the sandbox and survival genre. However, with such a multitude of players, communities have been formed with different purposes, such as Player Versus Player (PVP), redstone, and art.

The game starts and you spawn in a uniquely generated world. You have no weapons, no food, no materials, just a dream. The world is yours to shape.

Build. Explore. Survive.
