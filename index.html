Minecraft: An Indie Classic


With over 300 million downloads and 18 official versions, Minecraft is one of the most played and loved games of all time! First released in 2009 by Mohjang, the community has exploded over the years.

This game is primarily one of the sandbox and survival genre, however, with an expansive playerbase, communities have formed with different intrests, such as Player Versus Player (PVP), Redstone, and Building.

To start the game, you spawn in a uniquely generated world. You have no weapons, no food, no materials, just a dream. You know what you need to do, slay the final boss - the ender dragon.

Good luck.
